Abdullahi Abdulrasheed is the Founder and CEO of Whoisfr3e Clothing, a bold streetwear brand rooted in creativity, individuality, and freedom of expression. Launched in Abuja, Nigeria, Whoisfr3e was built on the belief that fashion is more than fabric. it’s a voice for those who refuse to be defined by limits.

From its first drop to its growing community across Africa and beyond, Whoisfr3e represents a culture of authenticity and self-belief. Under Abdullahi Abdulrasheed vision, the brand blends street culture, digital innovation, and storytelling to create apparel that speaks to a generation redefining freedom on its own terms.

Driven by passion, design, and purpose, Abdullahi Abdulrasheed continues to push the boundaries of modern streetwear proving that being fr3e isn’t just a look, it's a lifestyle.
